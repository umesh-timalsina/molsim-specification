Introduction
Provide a general speciﬁcation of a GPMSO molecular system and
the constructs necessary to accomplish this
Living document, can be updated over time
Base data structures that are general enough to support as many
chemical systems and interaction forms as needed
Two container objects required
System – Physical representations stored here
- Site, Connection, etc...
PotentialCollection – non-physical representations stored here
- Functional form of interaction, parameters, etc...
